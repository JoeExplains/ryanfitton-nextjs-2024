<p>This website is optimised for SEO and social media sites such as Twitter, by using Twitter Cards. The Bootstrap framework along with FontAwesome icons were used so the website can be viewed efficiently on a mobile device with responsive design.</p>
<p>The design is clean, simple and modern. By running on top of the WordPress Content Management System means the client can update and add pages or blog posts easily on their own.</p>
