<p>
  <a href="http://www.onethingiknow.co.uk/book/">One Thing I Know</a> is a book put together by
  selected organisations in the UK. The book features a large array of articles from creative
  leaders discussing interesting topics such as promoting your business and using social media
  effectively. The book itself is around 110 pages and features many outstanding illustrations by{' '}
  <a href="http://fiascodesign.co.uk/">Fiasco Design</a>.
</p>
<p>
  It&#8217;s free for anyone in the UK, the publishers only ask you to contribute a small amount of
  money for postage.
</p>
