<p>
  This was new to me; Google OptiScore is a new tool within your Google Ads portal which will
  suggest improvements which can be made across your whole account or individual campaign levels.
</p>
<p>The suggestions provided by OptiScore should improve your Ad performance for your site.</p>
<p>
  I watched a recent video provided by the Partners team at Google from their &#8216;
  <a href="https://events.withgoogle.com/elevenses/">Elevenses Livestream</a>&#8216;.
</p>

    <strong>What is an Optimisation score?</strong>
    <ul>
      <li style="list-style-type: none;">
        <ul>
          <li>
            Uses a combination of statistical models, simulations and machine learning to give you a
            personalised and real time recommendations tailored to your accountâ€™s performance.
          </li>
          <li>
            It is a score of 0 to 100, with 100 being the best it can be and 0 meaning the site
            needs improvement.
          </li>
          <li>
            OptiScore is a way to make your Google Ads account smarter. Use Smart Creatives, Smart
            Bidding and Smart Targeting.
          </li>
        </ul>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Benefits of OptiScore:</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>
        You should use OptiScore because it is Instant (the score updates and changes based on your
        changes), Personalised to you (you will only see a notice/suggestion if the system can see
        this will make a sizeable difference), Actionable and Scalable (you can view OptiScale
        across a whole account level or campaign level).
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Key Tips and Tricks:</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>
        Save time with writing your RSA

          <li>These can be templated, saving you time</li>

        </ul>
      </li>
    </ul>
